# Library

The **Library** tab shows all photos on your device in a grid view.

<img src={LocalPhotosImage} alt="Local Photos Screen" style={{width: '300px', height: 'auto'}} />

## Filters

Tap the **filter icon** (top right) to narrow down what's shown:

| Filter | Options |
|--------|---------|
| **Media** | All · Photos · Videos |
| **Sync** | All · Synced · Pending |
| **Hash** | All · Hashed · Unhashed |

Tap **Reset filters** to clear all active filters.

## Selection Mode

Long press any photo (or tap the **checkmark icon**) to enter selection mode:

- Tap photos to select/deselect them
- Tap the **grid icon** to select all pending photos at once
- Tap **Upload (N)** to sync only the selected photos
- Tap the **X icon** to exit selection mode

## Syncing Photos

<img src={SyncPhotosImage} alt="Sync Modal" style={{width: '300px', height: 'auto'}} />

Tap the **top left icon** to open the Sync panel. It shows:

- **Total** — all photos loaded
- **Hashed** — photos with SHA1 computed (used for deduplication)
- **Synced** — already on your PhotoPrism server
- **Pending** — not yet uploaded

From this panel you can **start hashing** (computes SHA1 for all local photos and checks the server for duplicates) or cancel an ongoing operation.

To upload, tap the **cloud icon** — this uploads all pending photos via WebDAV.

:::tip
Use **WiFi** when syncing large libraries to avoid mobile data usage.
:::

## Photo Badges

Each tile shows small indicators:

- **Sync badge** — bottom left: shows if the photo is synced or pending
- **Video duration** — bottom right: shown on video files
- **Live photo icon** — top right: shown on Live Photos
- **GIF icon** — top right: shown on animated GIFs
