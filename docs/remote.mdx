# Remote photos

Under the **Remote** tab, you can view photos stored on your PhotoPrism server.

  <img src={RemoteImage} alt="Remote Photos Screen" style={{width: '300px', height: 'auto'}} />
  
</div>

## Photos 

You can filter photos by:

- **Default**: View all photos
- **Review**: View photos pending review
- **Archived**: View archived photos
- **Private**: View private photos
- **Favorites**: View only favorite photos
- **OrderBy**: View photos ordered by newest,oldest,added,edited,name

## Media viewing

When viewing a photo, you can:
- Tap the photo to view it in full screen
- Tap the **Info** button to view photo details and metadata
- Tap the **Star** button to favorite the photo
- Tap the **Plus** button add the photo to a album
- Tap the **Archive** button to archive the photo
- Tap the **Delete** button to delete the photo
