# Albums

Albums are your curated photo collections synced from your PhotoPrism server. Access them from the **Explore** tab.

<img src={AlbumsImage} alt="Albums Screen" style={{width: '300px', height: 'auto'}} />

## Viewing Albums

Tap **Albums** in the Explore tab to open the albums list. Tap any album to browse its photos.

## Adding an Album

Tap the **+** button in the top right corner of the Albums screen.

## Favoriting an Album

Tap the **Star** icon on any album in the list to mark it as a favorite.

## Removing an Album

Tap the **Trash** icon on any album in the list to delete it from your server.
