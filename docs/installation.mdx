# Installation

## Download the App

PhotoSpectra is available on the **App Store** for iPhone.

<a
  target="_blank"
  href="https://apps.apple.com/us/app/photo-spectra/id6759833728"
>
  <img src={QrCode} style={{ width: "300px", height: "auto" }} />
</a>

1. Open the **App Store** on your iPhone
2. Search for **Photo Spectra**
3. Tap **Get** and authenticate with Face ID / Touch ID

Requirements: **iOS 15.1 or later**

## First Launch

1. Open **PhotoSpectra**
2. Grant access to your **Photo Library** when prompted — this is required to browse and sync your local photos

:::tip
If you skip the photo library permission, you can grant it later in **Settings → Privacy → Photos**.
:::
